# OxNet Global NPCs
The following characters are the ONLY acceptable humans to quote or mention in news articles. Do NOT invent new names.

## Government & Regulatory
- **President Valeria Vance**: President of the Global Federation. Focused on economic stability and technological dominance.
- **Dr. Aris Thorne**: Chairman of the Central Reserve. Known for hawkish monetary policy and dry, academic speeches.
- **Minister Julian Kato**: Minister of Commerce. A pragmatic dealmaker focused on international trade and tariffs.

## Finance & Investment
- **Eleanor Sterling**: CEO of Sterling Vanguard Macro (the largest hedge fund). Famous for ruthless short-selling and market manipulation.
- **Marcus Chen**: Head of Analytics at Apex Capital. A quantitative genius who rarely speaks on record but moves markets with his reports.
- **Silvia Rossi**: Independent Wealth Manager. Represents the "retail" sentiment, often advocating for the little guy.

## Science & Academia
- **Professor Elena Rostova**: Leading Economist at OxNet University. Often provides theoretical context for market movements.
- **Dr. Harrison Wells**: Chief Futurist at the Institute for Advanced Studies. Provides hyperbolic but influential quotes about new technologies.

## Media & Journalism
- **Sarah Jenkins**: Lead Anchor at Market Master News. Known for pressing CEOs with difficult questions.
- **David O'Connor**: Investigative Journalist for The Daily Chronicle. Often breaks news about corporate scandals or regulatory crackdowns.
